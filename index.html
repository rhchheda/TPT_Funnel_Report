Key Insights
What the data is telling us — and what to act on before team formation.
⚡
4 Teams Are Already Visible
Elder Detection, Dishwasher, Street Dogs, 3D Printer each have 3 interested students. Team formation can begin immediately for these 4 products.
⚠️
ECE Dominance — Act Now
67% of registrations are ECE. Cross-discipline mandate requires urgent outreach to CSE, ME, and MBA students before teams are locked.
🔧
Strong Build Capability
77% of students have HW or SW skills. 6 students are full-stack HW+SW — ideal for the hardware-intensive products like the oscilloscope and dishwasher.
💡
50% Brought Own Ideas
9 students came with their own problem statement — a strong signal of genuine product thinking. These students should be positioned as team leads.
🎓
4 Senior Students — Use Them
4 third-year students across AI&ML, ME, and AR. Each should lead one team — pairing them with 1st and 2nd year members creates the best learning dynamic.
🚀
6 Solo Ideas Need Homes
Bus Sensor, Oscilloscope, Fish Feeder, Night Light, Eco Route, AI Comm have 1 student each. Match them to existing teams or pair similar-domain solos together.
